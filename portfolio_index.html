WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO

ABOUT ME

Sun-kissed California beaches may have been my playground, but data became my calling. Santa Barbara offered the perfect blend: sunny days and the chance to pursue a greater education. I started at SBCC, where I earned my Associates in Computer Science. Then, I transitioned to UCSB to delve deeper into the world of data with a Bachelor's of Science in Statistics & Data Science. During my time there, I worked with the TAP Program, which empowers underrepresented students to transfer to universities and solidified my love for data. Juggling data for 200+ students honed my organizational and analytical skills. While diving into the world of data, I also rekindled my love for a sport that combines athleticism with strategy (volleyball, anyone?) and explored the artistic side with ceramics. Hot yoga kept my body in balance as my mind explored the intricacies of data. Now, I'm eager to leverage my data expertise and passion for making a difference.

WHY DATA SCIENCE?

My fascination with data science wasn't a sudden revelation, but rather a gradual realization. From a young age, I've always gravitated towards puzzles and intricate Lego creations. The satisfaction of meticulously piecing together the missing fragments and meticulously constructing elaborate structures fueled an insatiable desire to solve problems and understand how things work. Data science offered a powerful extension of this natural inclination, empowering me to tackle complex challenges on a much larger scale. Since then, I've meticulously cultivated a robust foundation in data science and analytics. Through coursework, projects, research, and industry experience, I've honed my skills in data cleaning, mastered various machine learning algorithms, and developed a passion for crafting impactful data visualizations. My ability to extract actionable insights from diverse datasets – financial records, healthcare data, marketing campaigns, even captivating images – positions me as a versatile asset in the ever-evolving world of information.
